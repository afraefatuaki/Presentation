Coffee with us is an Experience

Whether you are having your first morning cup to set the tone of the day or needing to regroup and push through the afternoon, we want you to experience a coffee that extracts the premium tones of the coffee bean. We use coffee beans that are ethically sourced with a rating of 85+. Combined with precise roasting measures, we are committed to the best flavor profile with every roast.

Colombian Coffee beans

Redber’s effort features beans produced in the Huila region of Colombia. All the coffee is ‘excelso’ grade stuff – which means medium and large sized beans that have been sorted by hand as quality control. It’s a mix of Caturra, Typica and Bourbon varieties grown between 1,200 and 2,000 metres above sea level.
